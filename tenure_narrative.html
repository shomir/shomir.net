Sharing My Narrative Statement for Tenure and Promotion to Associate Professor

Read This First

I am sharing a lightly edited version of my narrative statement for the benefit of junior faculty. I received tenure and promotion to associate professor during my university's 2023-2024 application cycle.

My application dossier contained itemized lists of accomplishments (prepared using our university's software for reporting faculty work), my CV, and a narrative statement.

I provide these companion notes and the statement as-is with no guarantees. In addition to reading materials like these, I recommend speaking with colleagues who can provide advice for your individual circumstances. Tenure committees also may share their views on what constitutes a strong narrative, and any advice from them should be taken seriously.Below is a version of my narrative statement that I've lightly edited for public release.
