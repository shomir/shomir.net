Need Some Help?

I receive many emails from prospective students who are interested in joining my lab. I use this page to respond to inquiries that have one or more of the following issues:

  • You did not follow the instructions on my website for contacting me
  • You sent an email that did not demonstrate knowledge of my research
  • You contacted me about an old affiliation that I no longer have
  • You appear to believe that I directly accept applications to a degree program
  • You asked a question that I answered in my Advising FAQ
  • You applied for a visiting position that does not exist
  • You appeared to contact several faculty members with a generic email

Many faculty members ignore emails with these issues. I decided that ignoring emails like yours would be mean, so I created this page to send as a reply. Do not be offended that I sent it to you.

You should check out my Advising FAQ for information on how to join my lab. You might also check out my Teaching FAQ for advice on emailing faculty. If you haven't already browsed my research and publications pages to learn about my research, you should do that too.

After reading those materials, if you sense a good match between our interests, you are welcome to carefully follow the instructions on my website to contact me again. Otherwise, there's no need to reply to this email.

Enjoy your day!
