About Me

Who I Am

I (he/him) am an associate professor in the Department of Human-Centered Computing and Social Informatics, part of the College of Information Sciences and Technology at Penn State. I also serve as the program coordinator for our B.S. in Information Technology Ethics and Compliance, and I lead the Human Language Technologies Lab, with research spanning natural language processing, privacy, security, and computational social science.

Contacting Me

You can reach me at shomir _at_ psu.edu, but if you are a student, see the instructions below.

  • If you're taking one of my classes, you might want to check out my Guide for Interacting With Faculty.
  • If you're seeking help with the Information Technology Ethics and Compliance major, speak with your academic advisor and follow their instructions before contacting me. Advising has access to information and options that are specific to your needs.
  • If you're interested in working with me on research, consult my Guide for Joining My Lab for how to contact me and what information to include.

My office is E310 Westgate Building.
